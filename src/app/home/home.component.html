Learn About Us

C4D is inspired by Zimbabweans who for more than 4 decades have watched politicians battling out at the political minefield that made development praxis null and void. Critical levers of development have been thrown aside. In all fairness, No sector has withstood the test of time and succeeded. No Zimbabwean has been spared harrowing experiences of life whether one is in diaspora, in world capitals, resides in sewage laden locations and suburbs, rural outposts in all the ten provinces of Zimbabwe. Even billionaires standing in world capitals feel ashamed to boast of nauseating wealthy amidst abject poverty characterizing Zimbabwe today. Itâ€™s an open secret that the suffering of Zimbabweans socially, economically, politically, culturally, intellectually and morally has scaled unprecedented heights to ignore. Someone at some point was going to shout for a stop, call development practitioners to attention, ask them if they see what is seen by everyone. That time has come. It is at this juncture, now, that C4D emerges, observant to the fact that practitioners can no longer afford to stand akimbo, fold their arms on their chests and look, let alone aside and claim all not to see anything wrong. Politicians have dominated Zimbabwean landscape for far too long. The situation obtaining in Zimbabwe compels development practitioners to come out of their shells, comfort zones and act. C4D will reach out and call upon individuals who feel compelled by the Zimbabwean stalled development case to converge with like-minded individuals and create a formidable garrison for hopeful Zimbabweans.
